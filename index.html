Meet Toby Sowery
At just 28 years old, **Toby Sowery** has made a remarkable impact on the racing world. From his early beginnings in **British F3** to his standout performances in the fiercely competitive **Indy Lights** series, Toby has consistently showcased his high-performance driving skills. Renowned for his precision, competitive spirit, and technical expertise, he embodies the future of British motorsport on the international stage.

Toby’s impressive career includes winning the **MSV F3 Cup Championship** on home soil and clinching the title in **India’s MRF Challenge** as he ascended the single-seater ladder, leading him to the **BRDC British F3 Championship**. In 2019, Toby finished an impressive third in his rookie season of **Indy Lights**, further establishing his reputation as a rising star.


Career Highlights So Far


    • 3rd in rookie season of Indy Lights (2019)
    • Champion of the MSV F3 Cup
    • Winner of India’s MRF Challenge
    • Participated in Italian F4, International GT Open, and Lamborghini Super Trofeo Asia
    • Maiden IndyCar Series test at Sebring (2023)
    • Competitive debut in Le Mans Prototypes (2023-24 Asian Le Mans Series)
